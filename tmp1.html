SOMNATH TEMPLE

The Somnath Temple is located in Prabhas Patan, near the city of Veraval in the Gir Somnath district of Gujarat, India.
It is situated on the western coast of the state, along the Arabian Sea.
City : The temple is near the city of Veraval.
Town/Locality: The temple is specifically situated in Prabhas Patan.
District : It is located in the Gir Somnath district.
State : The state is Gujarat.
