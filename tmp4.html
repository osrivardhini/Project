SRISAILA MALLIKARJUNA


Srisailam is located in the state of Andhra Pradesh, in the Kurnool district.
It is a significant pilgrimage center situated on the Nallamala Hills and is famous for the Bhramaramba Mallikarjuna Swamy Temple.
State: Andhra Pradesh
District: Kurnool
Significance: It is a major pilgrimage site for both Shaivism and Shaktism and is home to one of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva.
