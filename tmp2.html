KEDHARNATH TEMPLE


The Kedarnath Temple is located in the state of Uttarakhand, India.
It is situated in the Garhwal Himalayas, in the Rudraprayag district, near the head of the Mandakini River.
Location: The temple is in the Rudraprayag district of Uttarakhand.
Geography: It is in the Garhwal Himalayas, near the Mandakini River and the Chorabari Glacier.
Accessibility: It is approximately a 16 to 17 km trek from Gaurikund, the nearest road head.
