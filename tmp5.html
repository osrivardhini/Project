TRIMBAKESHWAR


The Trimbakeshwar Temple is located in the town of Trimbak, near Nashik in the state of Maharashtra, India.
It is situated approximately 28 km from Nashik city and is at the foothills of the Brahmagiri hills, which is the source of the Godavari River.
Town: Trimbak
District: Nashik District
State: Maharashtra, India
Nearest city: Nashik
Distance from Nashik: Approximately 28 km
Landmark: Located at the foot of the Brahmagiri hills
